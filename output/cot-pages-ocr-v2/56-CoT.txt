Through computer instruction you are able to
learn the language, which you find is similar to
English, so you are soon able to communicate with
your hosts.

They are not at all surprised to hear that you
arrived through the Cave of Time.

“You are not the first,”’ the head of the house-
hold tells you, “‘but we have visitors from other
times only once in a great while. When someone
comes, we are always glad to leam about life in
another era, because here we have achieved a sort
of paradise—we do not work, and the world is at
peace. It is a perfect society. That is why primitive
epochs, such as yours, interest us so much.”

If you stay in “the perfect society,”
tum to page 57.

If you try to retum to the Cave of Time,
tum to page 60.
